Chicken Fried Rice

  • 3 Cups cooked rice
  • Salt
  • 1 tablespoon garlic chopped
  • 1 tablespoon soya sauce organic
  • 1/4 teaspoon crushed pepper
  • 2 tablespoon oil
  • 1 to 2 green chilies
  • 2 springs onions
  • 1/4 to 1/2 cup bell peppers
  • 150 grams Chicken
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon soya sauce
  • 1 large pinch salt
  • 1 large pinch crushed pepper(or powder)
  1. Pour 2 tablespoon oil to a hot wok or pan.
  2. When the oil turns hot,transfer the chicken to the pan.
  3. Stir fry for 3 to 4 mintues until the chicken is almost cooked through.
  4. Move the chicken aside in the pan.Then beat the remaining egg and pour it.
  5. Scramble & cook the egg completely until it is firm but still soft.
  6. Remove the chicken & egg to a plate.
  7. Wipe off the wok or pan with a kitchen tissue.
  8. Pour another half tbsp oil to the same pan.
  9. Add garlic and fry for 30 seconds.
  10. Then add spring onion whites or scallions,chiles(optional)bell peppers and toss on a highest heat just for 2mins.
  11. The bell peppers must be crunchy and not soft.
  12. Transfer the rice,pepper powder and pour soya sauce along with any other sauce you desire.
  13. Mix and fry on the highest flame for a minute.
  14. Then add the chicken and egg.
  15. Continue to fry until the rice turns hot.This takes just a minute or two.
  16. Check salt and more if needed.Over frying will make the chicken fried rice hard.
  17. Serve chicken fried rice hot.
